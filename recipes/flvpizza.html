Extra flavored pizza

Description

Are you tired of the same boring taste in your pizza? Then try this dish in which we add a little homebrew taste to any kind of pizza, a flavor that surely will result in a refreshing new mix.

Ingredients

  • Any type of commercial pizza you can order to your home
  • Tomato ketchup
  • Crest toothpaste or any other brand (taste may vary)

Steps

  1. Order a pizza from your favorite store
  2. Take a slice of pizza
  3. Season to taste
  4. Done!!!
