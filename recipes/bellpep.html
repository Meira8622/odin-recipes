Froot Loops & milk

Description

Ideal recipe to start the day off, the milk and Froot Loops are a standard on the breakfast department but with the addition of bell peppers this recipe becomes more nutritious and versatile and at the same time leaves no dirty dishes to clean. The bitterness of the green bell peppers makes a perfect contrast to a usually sweet meal, but if needed could be replaced with a red bell pepper for a sweeter flavour.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1 cup of Froot Loops
  • 1 green bell pepper (or red bell pepper)

Steps

  1. Cut the bell pepper on the top side
  2. Pour the Froot Loops
  3. Pour the milk
  4. Mix
  5. Enjoy!
